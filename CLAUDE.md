# CLAUDE.md

This file provides guidance to Claude Code (claude.ai/code) when working with code in this repository.

## Project Overview

ESSD (Earth System Science Data) AI Competition project by team "AI Delinquents". Focus is on hydropower and water resources in the Pacific Northwest.

### Research Ideas Under Consideration

1. **Dam Removal Risk Assessment** (`idea1_dam_removal_risk.md`): Build a risk assessment system for dams that may face removal due to social, political, engineering, or environmental factors. Uses USGS streamflow data, FERC licensing docs, EIA energy data, census data, and environmental impact metrics.

2. **Wildfire Regime Shifts & Hydropower** (`idea2_fires_and_hydropower.md`): Analyze how shifting wildfire regimes affect hydropower basin stability in Washington state. Uses USGS wildfire data, NHD stream networks, watershed boundaries, and land cover data to classify risk levels and identify sediment/water quality impacts.

## Key Data Sources

- **USGS Water Data API**: https://api.waterdata.usgs.gov/
- **EIA Energy Data**: Forms 860, 923, 930 via https://catalyst.coop/pudl/
- **USGS Wildfire Dataset**: Combined wildland fire polygons 1835-2020
- **National Hydrography Dataset (NHD)**: Stream networks and catchments
- **HUC Watershed Boundaries**: HUC-8 or HUC-12 for dam locations
