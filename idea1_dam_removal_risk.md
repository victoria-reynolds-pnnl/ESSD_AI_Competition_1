# Idea 1 - streamflow data 

## Overall concept
Using streamflow data and other public resources, come up with a risk assessment for dams that may be at high risk of removal due to social, political, engineering, and/or environmental factors. Build a system that can assess the risk, and as well as identify missing data that is needed to compute a resonable risk profile.
